- **2.2.1-Newsletter**

- **2.2.2-Meeting-Agendas** *(placeholder)*

- **2.2.3-One-Pagers** *(placeholder)*  
  Reserved for narrative one-pagers and lightweight briefs you use to align leadership and teams on proposals and decisions.

- **2.2.4-Crisis-Management** *(placeholder)*  
  Reserved for comms playbooks and templates when things go wrong (incidents, outages, serious bugs) and you need to communicate quickly and clearly.

- **2.2.9-Courses**  
  Course design framework and template. Use this to structure internal enablement, PM onboarding, or external training materials in a repeatable way.

Typical use: **Newsletter** for steady-state updates, **Meeting-Agendas/One-Pagers** for focused sessions and proposals, **Crisis-Management** for high-pressure situations, and **Courses** for longer-form learning.
